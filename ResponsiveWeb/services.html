The Characters

  • Wolfal Elodie Hinsley

    Wolfal is a young female soldier and former royal from the planet Jew'ls. She has a very reserved personality and is usually more stoic in her behavior. She was born with the gifts of telepathy, telekensis, and persausion. She likes to study and use old fashioned fighting methods, like swordfighting. Many people across the Twenty-One Worlds tell tall tales about Wolfal, thus inspiring the name of the book series. She was named for the gray wolf, like most of her family, becuase it is the Jemish symbol of loyaty.

    Lawful Good

  • Jack-Caleb (JC) Stormhert

    JC Stormhert is a young Scientist and invetor from the Beginning World, aka the planet Earth. Unlike most Earthlings, JC has numerous powers or 'gifts' that caught the attetion of Wolfal when he was eleven years old. He is a genius inventor and scientist with the abilities to remember everything and control energy. He is Wolfal's greatest and most trusted ally. He has a very playful, chaotis personality, but is able to be a serious warrior when called to action.

    Chaotic Good

  • Sonjie Caleb Stormhert

    Sonjie is an SAIL, or a Stormhert Artifical Intellegent Lifeform, built by JC Stormhert to act as a younger brother and conpanion to Jack-Caleb. Created with the appearance and personailty of an eight year old child. He is playful and just as cirious as his creator. However, as the war starts, he becomes increasingly interested becoming a soldier like his creator. He was names Sonjie for the Jemish term 'Son Jee' which translates to 'Young Boy.' Sonjie is the narrator of the series, telling the story of the war many years after its conclusion.

    Neutral Good
