The Tale of Wolf is a Series currently being created by Faith E. Barrowclough about a war near the distant planet of Jew'ls. It follows the young protagonist, Wolfal E. Hinsley as she uncovers an acient legend and a dark conspiracy.

Our Audience

The stories in this series are meant to target the young adult audience. It wrestles with topics like war, tragedy, rebellion, and conflict, while also focusing on learning to trust, loyalty, friendship, and love.

Fantasy Vs. Sci-Fi Worldbuilding Elements

The Sci-Fi elements in this series are clear: with largely science focused elements to explain supernatural ablities, wonky inventions, space travel, and many examples of flawed futuristic societies. Meanwhile, swordfighting, royalty, and fantasy symbolism and elements are also embeded into the plot.

Mickal's Lullaby

Mickal's Lullaby is the first installation that will be coming out for this series. It focuses on younger, pre-soldier and pre-war Wolfal and her Jemish teacher, Feline Mickal as Wolfal learns more about her family history and her special abilities.
